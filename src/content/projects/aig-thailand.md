Deployed onsite to support AIG Thailand's digital transformation initiative, focusing on legacy system migration, customer portals, and marketing automation.

Integrated platform with LINE BCRM, Facebook Lead API, Salesforce REST API, and Adobe AEM 6.5, directly supporting digital marketing and lead generation campaigns.

Migrated legacy MS Access reporting to C# Windows Service, building multi-threaded services for insurance claims and customer registration workflows.

## Insurance Claim Document Submission Portal

Developed a nationwide WebApp enabling dealers across Thailand to submit insurance claim documents digitally, replacing manual paper-based processes.

Built responsive web interface with document upload, validation, and real-time status tracking. Integrated with backend systems for automated document routing and claim processing workflows.

Key features included multi-file upload, document type validation, dealer authentication, and admin dashboard for claim management and reporting.
